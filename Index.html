About

Building on a Legacy of Faith

Andrew Likoudis is the founder of the Likoudis Legacy Foundation, an ecumenical initiative dedicated to advancing theological scholarship and Christian unity. His work bridges academic theology, Catholic media, and strategic nonprofit leadership.

His latest book, Faith in Crisis (En Route Books, 2025), features a foreword by Rocco Buttiglione and contributions from Cardinal Robert Sarah, Mike Aquilina, Jimmy Akin, Tim Staples, and 30+ others. The volume has received endorsements from Cardinal Odilo Scherer, Rodrigo Guerra, and Cardinal Christophe Pierre.

He is the grandson of James Likoudis (1928–2024), President Emeritus of Catholics United for the Faith, and nephew of Paul Likoudis, longtime editor of The Wanderer.
